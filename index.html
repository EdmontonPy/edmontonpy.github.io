    Welcome to the Edmonton Python Meetup Group.

    Started in April 2013, we meet on the second Monday of each month at 7pm in the Startup Edmonton offices (#301, 10359-104 St). Our next meeting is on May 13, 2013. If you are coming, please let us know on our Meetup page.

    We hope to foster a strong, supportive, and proficient Python community in the Edmonton area. Everyone interested in any aspect of Python development is welcome to join.

    Each week we will listen to presentations and discuss Python news and events.

    Past Presentations

    Wherever possible, code and slides from past presentations is made available in our GitHub repository.

    Contacts

    You can contact the organizers at organizers@EdmontonPy.com
